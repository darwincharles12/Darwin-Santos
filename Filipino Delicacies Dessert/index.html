~ TOP BEST SELLERS ~

Puto Bumbong

Puto-bumbong is a steamed purple rice cake that is commonly sold during Christmas season. The mixture is inside grease bamboo tubes and steamed. After steaming the puto bumbong is placed in banana leave and topped with grated coconut and brown sugar.

Halo-Halo

Halo-halo is a popular cold dessert made with crushed ice, evaporated milk, and mixed ingredients like, ube, leche flan, sago, gulan, stripped coconut, and pinipig. It is serve with a ube in top. It is usually prepared in a tall clear glass and a long spoon.

Bibingka

Puto Bibingka, a Filipino baked rice cake, is typically consumed for breakfast or as a merienda, especially around Christmas time. It is traditionally prepared in a terracotta oven coated with banana leaves.
